Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 14, 2024

1. Introduction

Welcome to Kasiko (the "Bot"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled in a safe and responsible manner. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our Discord bot.

Please read this Privacy Policy carefully. By using Kasiko, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information to provide and improve our services:

  • Discord User ID: A unique identifier for your Discord account.
  • Server ID: A unique identifier for the Discord server where the bot is used.
  • Server Owner ID: The unique identifier for the owner of the Discord server.
  • Email Address: Collected only if you purchase the Royal Pass.

Additionally, we use data provided by third-party services such as Discord.js, Render, Giphy, and various npm packages to operate and enhance the bot's functionality.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

  • Providing Services: To operate and maintain Kasiko, including responding to commands and delivering features.
  • Improving Services: To understand how users interact with the bot and to enhance functionality and user experience.
  • Communication: To send important updates, respond to support requests, and provide information about your purchases (e.g., Royal Pass).
  • Security: To monitor and prevent unauthorized access or misuse of the bot.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties, except in the following circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We may share information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating the bot, conducting our business, or serving our users, provided that these parties agree to keep this information confidential.
  • Legal Requirements: We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or a portion of our assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

We ensure that all third-party service providers adhere to strict confidentiality and data protection standards.

5. Data Security

We implement a variety of security measures to maintain the safety of your personal information. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

6. Data Retention

We retain your information only for as long as necessary to provide you with our services and to comply with our legal obligations. When your information is no longer needed, we will securely delete or anonymize it.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access: The right to request access to your personal information.
  • Correction: The right to request correction of any inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Deletion: The right to request deletion of your personal information under certain circumstances.
  • Objection: The right to object to the processing of your personal information.
  • Data Portability: The right to request a copy of your information in a structured, machine-readable format.

If you wish to exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information provided in the "Contact Us" section below.

8. Third-Party Services

Kasiko integrates with the following third-party services:

  • Discord.js: A powerful library for interacting with the Discord API.
  • Render: Currently used for hosting the bot.
  • Giphy: Used to provide GIFs for related commands.
  • npm Packages: Various packages to enhance functionality.

These third-party services may have their own privacy policies governing their use of your information. We encourage you to review their policies to understand how your data is handled.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the "Last Updated" date will be revised accordingly. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us through the following channels:

  • Email: kasikoinc@gmail.com
  • Website: https://kasiko-bot.vercel.app/
  • Official Kasiko Discord Server: Join our Discord Server

11. Children's Privacy

Kasiko is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately, and we will take steps to delete such information.

12. International Users

Kasiko is operated from India and is intended for users globally. By using the Bot, you consent to the transfer and processing of your information in India and any other countries where our service providers are located.

Thank you for using Kasiko! We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring a safe and enjoyable experience.
